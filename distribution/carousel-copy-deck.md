## Design Guardrails

- Use the original crown/fitted/concrete visual language.
- No Knicks/NBA logos.
- No player likeness.
- No official jerseys, uniform trade dress, or numbers.
- Keep text large and sparse.
- Use black, white, concrete gray, restrained orange, and restrained blue.
